Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to MarketPlaxe.com. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access our website, browse marketplace listings, create an account, or make a purchase.

By using MarketPlaxe.com, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our marketplace.

Personal Information

Depending on your interaction with our website, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing Address
  • Shipping Address
  • Order Details
  • Account Login Information
  • Communication Preferences
Automatically Collected Information

When visiting our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Pages Viewed
  • Products Viewed
  • Search History
  • Shopping Cart Activity
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process marketplace transactions
  • Manage customer accounts
  • Improve website functionality
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Process returns and refunds
  • Send shipping updates
  • Personalize shopping experiences
  • Detect fraudulent activity
  • Improve marketplace performance
  • Send promotional emails (with your consent)
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

MarketPlaxe uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember shopping preferences
  • Maintain your shopping cart
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze visitor behavior
  • Display relevant marketplace offers
  • Enhance user experience

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, although certain features may not function correctly.

4. Marketplace Sellers

To complete transactions, certain order details may be shared with participating sellers solely for:

  • Order fulfillment
  • Shipping
  • Customer support
  • Return processing

Marketplace sellers are expected to handle customer information responsibly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

5. Payment Information

Payments are securely processed through trusted third-party payment providers.

MarketPlaxe does not store complete payment card information on its servers.

Supported payment methods may include:

  • Visa
  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Other approved payment services

6. Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may send:

  • Marketplace updates
  • New product announcements
  • Seasonal promotions
  • Exclusive discounts
  • Shopping recommendations

You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in our emails.

7. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only with trusted service providers necessary to operate our marketplace, including:

  • Payment processors
  • Shipping providers
  • Marketplace sellers
  • Website hosting services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Analytics providers
  • Fraud prevention services

8. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable safeguards to help protect your information, including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Secure payment processing
  • Access controls
  • Routine security monitoring
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

While we strive to protect your information, no online transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Update account details
  • Request deletion of eligible personal information
  • Withdraw marketing consent
  • Request information regarding data processing

10. Children's Privacy

MarketPlaxe is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates become effective immediately upon publication on this page.
