Return & Exchanges

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

www.marketplaxe.com is an online product discovery platform and does not currently sell products or process orders directly. This Returns & Exchange Policy sets out the general standards we expect from the third-party sellers and retailers featured on our platform, and explains how to raise a return, exchange, or refund request for a purchase made through a linked seller.

By using www.marketplaxe.com to discover products, you agree that the terms outlined in this policy apply once online ordering becomes available, and that in the meantime any purchase is completed directly with the third-party seller or retailer, whose own return policy governs that purchase.

1. Return Eligibility

Most eligible products may be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.

To qualify for a return, items must:

  • Be unused and in their original condition.
  • Include all original packaging, accessories, manuals, and tags.
  • Not show signs of wear, damage, or misuse.
  • Be accompanied by proof of purchase or order confirmation.

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene, safety, and legal reasons, certain products cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

  • Personal care products.
  • Customized or personalized items.
  • Gift cards and digital products.
  • Perishable goods.
  • Products marked as final sale or non-returnable.

3. Exchange Policy

Eligible products may be exchanged for another size, color, or replacement, subject to stock availability.

If the requested replacement is unavailable, customers may choose between:

  • A refund to the original payment method.
  • A replacement with a similar product where available.
  • Store credit where permitted.

4. Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or you receive the wrong item, please contact us as soon as possible after delivery.

To help us resolve the issue quickly, please include:

  • Your order number.
  • A description of the issue.
  • Clear photographs of the product and packaging.

Where verified, we will arrange an appropriate replacement, exchange, or refund in accordance with applicable law.

5. Refund Process

After the returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you regarding the outcome of your refund request.

If approved:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
  • Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.
  • Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the return results from our error or a defective product.

6. Return Shipping

Customers may be responsible for return shipping costs unless:

  • The item was damaged during delivery.
  • An incorrect product was shipped.
  • The product is confirmed to be defective.

We recommend using a trackable shipping service for valuable returns.

7. product discovery platform Seller Returns

Some products sold by independent product discovery platform sellers may have return conditions that differ from this general policy.

Where applicable, any seller-specific return requirements will be clearly displayed on the product page before purchase.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received your refund after confirmation, we recommend:

  • Checking your bank or payment account.
  • Contacting your credit card provider.
  • Contacting your financial institution, as processing times may vary.

If you still require assistance, please contact our support team.

9. Policy Updates

MarketPlaxe reserves the right to modify this Returns & Exchange Policy at any time to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes.

Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
